Mixed Strength Club

🏋 🏅 💪

The club's goal is to develop a number of strength-based competitions in which everyone can compete for prizes. The competition ideas will be voted on, so they will be diverse enough that everyone can challenge themselves and learn about new challenges. Unlike other sports clubs, this one is designed to be inclusive, with the goal of providing a diverse range of strength sports in which anyone can have a chance to win. Furthermore, we want to bring together people with diverse backgrounds so that we can enrich ourselves by learning more about various strength-based challenges and competitions.
